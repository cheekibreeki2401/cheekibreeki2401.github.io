OUR AMAZING SOFTWARE

With a combined 50 years of experience creating and maintaining software that sees every day use in coprorate environments and personal use, we have devised software that is of the utomost quality and is contributed to by hundreds of other software programmers every year to create the best software for your needs. Our software is maintained under the principles of the Free Open Source Software foundation and the GNU project. We are not averse to proprietary software, not at all, but prefer to see a world where contributions and community focus are encouraged. Please, check out our amazing software including the one to kick it all off, the Universal Tagging System

MEET THE TEAM

Edge Software is committed to a diverse range of people and ideas to create what we and our users consider the best software.
