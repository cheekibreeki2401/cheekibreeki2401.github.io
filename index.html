Welcome to 100Smiles

Locals looking after locals, that's what we promise. A family dental practice operating for over 50 years, we have made it our mission to provide the best dental care for the region with our trademark smiles as a goal for us to strive for with each and every patient.

A practice for the whole family

Is little Suzie becoming a big girl with that awkward time of her life where her teeth don't quite line up quite right, are your parents getting to the age where they need to have dentures or are you looking for that time after work to get that regular check up in? Fear not, we at 100Smiles dentists cater to the needs of the whole family to keep them smiling!

Our qualified staff

We at 100Smiles dentists only hire the most qualified and proffesional but also down to Earth staff to make your time sitting at the chair as painless as possible. Orthodontic specialists, general practioner of dentistry and a friendly administration staff ensure the proper care for you and your family's teeth.
